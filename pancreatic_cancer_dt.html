The Dataset
Debernardi et al. 2020 — 590 patients from 4 UK & Spain hospitals. 3 classes: Control (183), Benign (208), PDAC Cancer (199). All clinically annotated urine samples.
⚙️
The Model
Decision Tree Classifier (sklearn). max_depth=4, Gini criterion, min_samples_leaf=10. Trained on 80% of data (472 patients). Validated with 5-fold cross-validation.
🎯
Why Decision Tree?
Fully interpretable — every decision is traceable. Ideal for medical AI where transparency and clinical trust matter more than raw accuracy alone.
